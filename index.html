Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic game that can be played by two players. The game is played on a 3x3 grid, with each player taking turns placing their symbol (usually X or O) in an empty space on the board. The goal of the game is to get three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does.

How to play:

  1. The first player places their symbol in an empty space on the board.
  2. The second player then takes their turn and places their symbol in an empty space on the board.
  3. Players continue taking turns until one player gets three of their symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  4. If neither player has won and there are no more empty spaces on the board, the game ends in a tie.
